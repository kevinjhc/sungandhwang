Real Estate Transactions

Sung Hwang & Kim LLP represents clients in a wide range of real estate transactional matters, including purchase and sale agreements, financing, title search, title claims and title transfer, real estate settlements, lease agreements, and landlord and tenant issues. We provide such services for both commercial and residential properties, and our extensive experience provides a strong foundation for our understanding of various legal and practical issues that are specific to the industries and locales in the greater Washington, DC area.

Our clients rely on the firm's knowledgeable attorneys and staff during complex real estate transactions to make the experience as seamless as possible. We assist our clients in reviewing and preparing required documents as well as in understanding the intricacies involved in complex real estate transactions.
